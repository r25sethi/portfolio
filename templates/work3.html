3 : BRIDGE DESIGN PROJECT

In 1B, I worked along with 2 other team-mates and designed a bridge made up of trusses and gausset plates. We came up with 3 different designs and chose the cheapest one We did all manual calculations and confirmed each other's designs. The bridge was designed to cover 14 m, with a pin at one end and a roller at the other. The project met all the requirements and constraints.

"This design project helped me enhance my understanding of trusses and how to efficiently combine ideas of a team."
