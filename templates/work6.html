6 : TRON DAYS

Tron Days were very beneficial in helping me understand the functioning of servo motors. Through this project, I was able to understand how controller configuration worked and how essential it was to set the motors in the right orientation. We had to design a functioning robot that picked up artifacts from the ground and place them on a stand ahead, using a controller. I also understood the application on a pnuematic piston.

"An essential project for incoming engineering students to understand and apply motor skills in an activity like this. Had to perform under a time constraint and build the robot from scratch. "
