5 : TEK STORE

Tek Store was a custom designed software ready for use in an inventory based tech store. All the code was in C++ and used files and the concept of efficient run times. The software consisted of a manager, employee and customer platform. The manager could edit details and overall inventory count, while the employee could store customer details for sale/return and keep a track of the updated prices. The customer was able to view all the items available, based on category selected and note the prices. The customer also had an option to indicate his intention to purchase to the employee.
