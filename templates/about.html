3 : About me

Education

Currently attending University of Waterloo to complete a degree in the field of Mechatronics Engineering and have studied various courses like Data Structures, Static Analysis, Material Selection, Chemistry for Engineers, etc. I also completed an AutoCAD course from Britain International Academy and graduated from Carmel School Kuwait in April 2019.

Key Skills and Qualifications

Certified to use the Machine Shop, completed WHMIS, Worker Health, Safety and Awareness, knowledge of GD&T, can interpret engineering drawings, proficient in SolidWorks, AutoCAD, C++, Python, MS Office and truss analysis. I also have experience in assembly and disassembly of products and have used several power tools to machine some parts for my design team- UW Rover Robotics.

Interests

Mechanically inclined, enthusiastic and motivated student. I am interested in working with machines and enhancing them for the future, making them more efficient and financially viable.
